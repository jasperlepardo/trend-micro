How to find the activation code for your product.

An Activation Code (AC) is used to activate your Trend Micro product. Without the AC, these products will still be installed, but their update and scanning features will be disabled.

It consists of two letters followed by four numbers, and then by five groups of five numbers, or 37 digits in all, including hyphens.

The AC is typically received once you register your product.
